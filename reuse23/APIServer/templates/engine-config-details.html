JavaScriptCore

JavaScriptCore (jsc) is Apple's engine for the Safari web browser. We used three tiering configurations for testing: jsc-int which has JITs disabled and only used the rewriting interpreter, jsc-bbq which has the interpreter and the BBQ quick JIT, and jsc-omg which has the interpreter and the OMG optimizing JIT
